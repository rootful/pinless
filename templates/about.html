About Pinless

Pinless is a privacy-focused frontend for Pinterest. It allows you to browse public content on Pinterest without exposing your IP address or being tracked by Pinterest.

FAQ

I want to report abuse

Pinless doesn't host any content - it just proxies what's already publicly available on Pinterest. If something needs to be taken down, please report it directly to Pinterest.

Why cookies?

URLs with tokens end up in your history and server logs. Session cookies are more private in this case.
